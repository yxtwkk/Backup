数据集

我们采用真实数据集来评价DistStream，真实数据集均来自UCI Machine Learning Repository。我们选择了三个具有不同维度的包涵聚类标签的真实数据集KDDCUP99，CoverType和KDDCUP98。这三个数据集都曾在不同的经典的聚类算法的论文中使用过。三个数据集分别具有不同的特点。

【1】KDD-CUP’ 99 Network Intrusion Detection dataset consists of a series of TCP connection records from two weeks of LAN network traffic managed by MIT Lincoln Labs. Each n record can either correspond to a normal con- nection, or an intrusion or attack. The attacks fall into four main categories: DOS (i.e., denial-of-service), R2L (i.e., unauthorized access from a remote machine), U2R (i.e., unauthorized access to local supe- ruser privileges), and PROBING (i.e., surveillance and other probing). As a result, the data contains a total of five clusters including the class for “normal connections”. The attack-types are further classified into one of 24 types, such as buffer-overflow, guess-passwd, nep- tune, portsweep, rootkit, smurf, warezclient, spy, and so on. It is evident that each specific attack type can be treated as a sub-cluster. Most of the connections in this dataset are normal, but occasionally there could be a burst of attacks at certain times. Also, each con- nection record in this dataset contains 42 attributes, such as duration of the connection, the number of data bytes transmitted from source to destination (and vice versa), percentile of connections that have “SYN” errors, the number of “root” accesses, etc.

根据[1] 描述，KDD CUP’99 Network Intrusion Detection数据集来自MIT林肯实验室监控的两周LAN网络流量的TCP连接记录，数据具有变化性和波动性。数据集共有4898431条数据，包含“正常连接”和“四个攻击类型”共5个类。每条连接记录包含42个属性，包含：TCP连接时间，source到end的传输数据量等。根据[1]，实际聚类过程中，将利用42个属性中的34个属性。

【2】Covertype contains cartographic data about some wilderness areas inside the Roosevelt National Forest of northern Colorado. The leading thought of analyzing this dataset is to be able to predict the forest cover type of specific regions from car- tographic variables, which is a classification task. After removing the classification attribute, 581,012 data points in 54 dimensions remained.

CoverType Forcast数据是描述some wilderness areas inside the Roo- sevelt National Forest of northern Colorado的cartographic 数据，共有581012条数据。剔除掉分类属性后，数据共有54个维度。

实验环境

我们搭建了10台机器作为本地集群。我们采用Spark standalone模式，其中，1台机器作为master，8台机器作为slave，作为计算平台。计算平台的8个slave节点接收来自kafka的消息并进行计算。

此外，8台slave节点同时作为kafka的数据发送端（生产者），以此构建分布式消息分发平台。由10台机器共同构成hdfs ，作为存储平台。

每台机器的硬件环境为4核Intel(R) Core(TM) i7-6700K处理器，64GB内存和1.8TB硬盘。软件环境为Ubuntu 16.04.3 LTS操作系统，spark版本为2.4.0，kafka版本为0.10.0.1，java版本为1.8，scala版本为2.11.8。

评价指标和方法

本文根据MOA实现了标准单机版Clustream,Denstream,DStream和Clustree作为baseline。我们将本文框架下的对应的分布式算法和对应的单机版进行对比，通过比较二者的吞吐率和聚类效果，说明DistStream框架在不影响聚类效果的情况下，可以得到吞吐率的大幅度提升。

聚类效果 Cluster Quality

根据[4][5]提供的评价方法，为了描述聚类效果随着数据流入的变化，我们设定设定阈值h=50000条数据，即每流入50000条数据，我们对现有的微簇集合进行offline聚类，并用对聚类结果其进行评价。阈值的选择并不会影响到聚类的最终效果。

The commonly used cluster quality evaluation metrics fail to take the evolution of streams and the freshness of instances into account. Take the F-measure as an example.The merge or split of clusters retrieved from stream points could be considered as false positive or false negative. In our
evaluation, we use a recently proposed CMM (Clustering Mapping Measure) criterion , which is external criterion taking into account the age of objects. The CMM measure can reflect errors related to emerging, splitting, or moving clusters, which are situations inherent to the streaming context. The CMM measure is a combination of penalties for each one of the following faults: 1) Missed objects. Clusters that are constantly moving may eventually lose objects, and thus CMM penalizes for these missed objects; 2) Misplaced objects. Clusters may eventually overlap over the course of the stream, and thus CMM penalizes for misplaced objects; 3) Noise inclusion. CMM penalizes for noisy objects being inserted into existing clusters. Basically, the CMM valuen ranges from 0 to 1. Larger CMM value indicates higher cluster quality, while smaller CMM value indicates lower cluster quality.

变化的数据流聚类是随着数据的流入不断变化的，并且流入系统的数据点权重随着时间的推移而不断变小，这种特性导致在数据流聚类中可能产生稳定数据凸数据聚类不会产生的错误：（1）归属错误的数据点 misplaced objects；（2）错过的数据点 missed objects ；（3）噪声数据点 noise。在对聚类结果进行评价时，需要考虑以上的三种错误类型以及数据点的权重随着时间衰减的特性。

常用的聚类评价方法，比如SSQ，F-meausure等不能很好的对具有变化数据流的数据聚类进行评价[5]。Clustering Mapping Measure（CMM）[6]将算法得到的聚类结果与ground truth聚类进行匹配，每个found聚类都匹配到最相近的ground truth聚类，并且比对found聚类和ground truth聚类的差异。CMM是对三种错误的惩罚归一化之和，因此CMM的值域在[0,1]之间，且CMM越大，代表聚类效果越好。如果没有错误发生，则CMM为1。

$CMM(C,CL) = 1-\frac{\sum_{o \in F }w(o)·pen(o,C)}{\sum_{o \in F}w(o) ·con(o,Cl(o))}$

吞吐率 Throughput

【2】To run our algorithm on really huge datasets, we created the Cartesian product of the Tower and Covertype dataset. In this way, we got a naturally structured dataset that is large enough to test the ability of our algorithm to handle huge amounts of data. We used a 1.5GB-sized subset of the Cartesian product consisting of 11,620,300 data points with 57 attributes, which we refer to as the BigCross dataset.

为了测试框架在大规模数据集上的处理能力，我们在进行吞吐率和扩展性测试时，对数据数目小于百万级的数据进行扩展，通过replay原始数据集的方式，保证在测试期间，每个数据集流入系统的数据可以保证在500万左右[2]。

吞吐率表示单位时间内算法处理数据的能力，是一个统计意义上的平均值。实际测试过程中，我们利用spark streaming提供的两个监控metirc：数据量numRecords，处理数据所需时间processingDelay进行计算。

$ThroughPut = \frac{numRecords}{processingDelay}​$

扩展性

扩展性在本文中表示随着计算节点（并行度）的增加，DistStream的加速比。通过对比在不同计算节点下，DistStream的吞吐率，进而反映DistStream的扩展性。$ThroughPut_P$表示在P并行度下，DistStream的吞吐率。

$S_p = \frac{ThroughPut_1}{ThroughPut_P}​$
